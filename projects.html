Jcube Basketball League

JCube Basketball League is an organization who brings basketball players around metro manila together. Whether you are a competitive player or a recreational player or even the type of player that uses the game of basketball as an exercise this organization is for you. Currently they have 2 types of tournament. First is the Commercial Cup, where you get to build your own team to join or join the other team if you are a free agent. Second is the Camaraderie Cup, where you got to meet other players. This type of tournament have drafting type where you are all free agent and the team captains get to choose their teammates. The organizers see to it that the teams were balanced to have a great basketball games. Also you get to play with the organizer since this type of tourney promotes camaraderie and frienship with your co-players and builds brotherhood of the same passion. Ball is life.

This project used HTML, CSS and Bootstrap for Front-end and PHP, MySQL and JavaScript/JQuery for the Back-end. This project also used MVC Laravel 5.7 version framework.
